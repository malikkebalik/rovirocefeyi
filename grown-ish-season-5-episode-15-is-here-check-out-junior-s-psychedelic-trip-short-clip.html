Grown-ish Season 5, Episode 15 is Here Check Out Juniors Psychedelic Trip Short Clip

August 26, 2024
blog

On Wednesday, Freeform released a clip from grown-ish Season 5 Episode 15 titled “Junior’s Psychedelic Trip.” In the episode, Junior, Annika and Kiela experiment with shrooms which leads to some trippy visions.

The series follows Yara Shahidi as Zoey, Dre and Rainbow’s 17 year old daughter, as she moves away from home for college and quickly discovers that things don’t always go her way. It is executive produced by Kenya Barris and explores topics concerning higher education such as racial injustice, mental health and sexuality.

The episode also stars Marcus Scribner (Andre Jr.), Trevor Jackson (Aaron Jackson), Francia Raisa (Ana Torres), Emily Arlook (Nomi Segal) and Jordan Buhat (AJ). Fans can catch new episodes of “grown-ish” Wednesdays at 10:30/9:30c on Freeform or stream the next day on Hulu.

This week, viewers get to see what happens when Junior and his friends decide to take a trip down a psychedelic rabbit hole. Tune in to find out!
