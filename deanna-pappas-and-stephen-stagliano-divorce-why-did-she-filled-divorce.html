Who is Deanna Pappas?

DeAnna Marie Stagliano is a well-known American TV personality who rose to prominence after appearing on seasons 11 and 4 of The Bachelor and The Bachelorette, respectively, on ABC.

Pappas, the middle of three children, is of Greek descent and was born in Marietta, Georgia before her family relocated to Kentucky. At the age of twelve, she and her siblings Thomas and Chrissy moved to Newnan, Georgia to live with their father after their mother passed away from cancer. She formerly lived in Music City. Soon after finishing high school, she enrolled at Georgia Military College, where she eventually earned a BA in General Studies.

Who is Deanna Pappas’ Husband?

Stephen Stagliano, whose brother Michael Stagliano appeared on season five of The Bachelorette, proposed to Pappas in August 2010. They tied the knot in Georgia on October 22nd, 2011. Their little girl Addison Marie was born on February 6, 2014. They welcomed a boy on March 1, 2016, and named him Austin Michael.
