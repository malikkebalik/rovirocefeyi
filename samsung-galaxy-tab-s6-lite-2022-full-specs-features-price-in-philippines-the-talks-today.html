Here’s The Complete List of Specifications, Features, & Price of the Samsung Galaxy Tab S6 Lite (2022)

The Samsung Galaxy Tab S6 Lite (2022) was officially announced on May 14, 2022, and released on May 23, 2022. The tablet is fueled with a non-removable Li-Po 7040 mAh battery + 15W wired charging. It is available in various colors like Chiffon Pink, Angora Blue, and Oxford Gray.

Body & Display:

The dimension of the tab is 244.5 x 154.3 x 7 mm and weighs 465/467 grams (1.03 lb). It comes with a screen size of 10.4 inches TFT LCD capacitive touchscreen display resolution of 1200 x 2000 pixels, and a 5:3 aspect ratio. It is built with a glass front, aluminum back, and an aluminum frame.

Platform:

The tab is powered by Qualcomm SM7150 Snapdragon 732G (8 nm) Octa-core processor coupled with Adreno 618 graphics processing unit (GPU). It runs on Android 12, upgradable to Android 13 + One UI 5 operating system. It comes in variants such as 64GB + 4GB RAM and 128GB + 4GB RAM.

Main Camera:

The rear side of the Samsung Galaxy Tab S6 Lite (2022) sports an 8-megapixel (wide) camera with features like HDR and panorama.

Selfie Camera:

For clicking selfies and self-shooting videos, there is a single camera on the front that features a 5-megapixel (wide) camera.

Connectivity options on the device include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, dual-band, Wi-Fi Direct, Bluetooth 5.0, A2DP, LE, GPS, GLONASS, BDS, GALILEO, QZSS, and USB Type-C. Its sensors include accelerometer, proximity, gyro, Samsung DeX, and ANT+.
