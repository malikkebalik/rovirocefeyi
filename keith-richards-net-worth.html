Keith Richards Wiki Biography

Keith Richards is a legendary singer and songwriter, who is famous for being a part of the world-famous band called “The Rolling Stones”. He is considered to be one of the best guitarists in the history of rock. During his career, Keith has been nominated for and has won many different awards. Some of them include Grammy Award, Scream Award, Shockwaves NME Award and others. In addition to this Keith has also appeared in different movies, for example ‘Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides”, “Man on Horseback”, “Rolling Stones: One More Shot” and “Pirates of the Caribbean: At World’s End”.

Keith Richards Net Worth $340 Million

So how rich is Keith Richards? It is said that Keith’s net worth is $340 million. This sum might change in the future, as he still continues his career.

Keith Richards was born in 1943, in England. From a very young age, Keith became interested in music and his mother bought him his first guitar. Keith admired such artists as Scotty Moore, Billie Holiday, Duke Ellington and Louis Armstrong. Later Richards met Mick Jagger and became friends with him. Soon Keith became a part of the band called “Little Boy Blue and the Blue Boys”. “The Rolling Stones” was formed in 1962. There were few past members in this group and now it consists of Keith, Mick Jagger, Ronnie Wood, Darryl Jones and Charlie Watts. Keith has not only played the guitar in the band, but has also written many popular songs usually in conjunction with Mick Jagger, and this has had a huge impact on the growth of Keith Richards’ net worth. “The Rolling Stones” has released many albums, some of them being “Sticky Fingers”, “Some Girls”, “Undercover”, “Dirty Work”, “Black and Blue” and many others. All these albums added a lot to Keith’s net worth.

In addition to this, Keith is also known as a solo artist. He released his first solo single in 1978 and gained a lot of popularity with it. In 1988 he released his first studio album, entitled “Talk Is Cheap”. Richards’ second album was released in 1992 and was entitled “Main Offender”. These albums proved that Keith was able to perform as a solo artist as well. Of course, his solo performances have also had an impact on the growth of Keith Richards’ net worth. During his career, Keith has also worked with many different artists, for example John Phillips, Billy Preston, Max Romeo, Chuck Berry, Ronnie Spector and many others.

All in all, it could be said that Keith Richards is one of the best musicians of all time. He has a lot of experience and is still continuing his career. Many artists look up to him and are influenced by his work and success. Together with “The Rolling Stones, ” he has become legendary in the music industry. What is more, Keith has worked with other artists and helped them to become more popular and create incredible music.
