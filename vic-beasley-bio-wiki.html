Who is Vic Beasley?

Vic Beasley is an American football player. He currently plays as an outside linebacker for the Atlanta Falcons of the National Football League (NFL).

After playing college football for Clemson University, Beasley was drafted by the Falcons as the eighth overall pick in the 2015 NFL Draft.

Vic Beasley’s Early life, childhood, and education

Beasley was born on July 8, 1992, in Adairsville, Georgia, USA. There is no information about his parents.

He is an American by nationality and he is of African-American ethnicity.

Beasley attended Adairsville High School in Adairsville, Georgia, where he was a three-sport standout in football, basketball, and track.

He played as running back and linebacker for the Adairsville Tigers High School football team. He was named All-county and All-area in both his junior and senior seasons.

At the 2010 GHSAA Regional 7-AA Championships, he placed third in the high jump and second in the triple jump.

He was listed as the No. 19 athlete in the nation in 2010. After high school, he played college football at Clemson University.
