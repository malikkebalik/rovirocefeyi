She’s known for her stunning looks and completely unfiltered personality.

It comes after Nikki made a new life for herself in Queensland as a way of escaping the brutal lockdowns which have gripped Victoria.

‘It’s really depressing down here. All our friends’ businesses are going bust and all my industry mates can’t work… it’s depressing watching your kids staring at a screen or a wall,’ she said at the time.
