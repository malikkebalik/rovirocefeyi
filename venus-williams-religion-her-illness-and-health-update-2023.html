Update on Venus Williams’s Health and Illnesses for 2023

Venus Williams, an American tennis player, announced she has Sjögren’s syndrome, making headlines.

Sjögren’s syndrome, which is often spelled “Showgren’s syndrome,” is a chronic systemic autoimmune disorder.

It happens when the immune system of the body unintentionally attacks itself and damages healthy tissues.

It is chronic because it lasts a very long time and impacts several organs. When Sjögren’s syndrome first appears, it usually does so independently.

When Sjögren’s syndrome manifests alongside another autoimmune disease like lupus, rheumatoid arthritis, or systemic sclerosis, it is referred to as secondary or related to the condition.

Currently, there is no cure for this illness.
