Who Is Frankie Jonas? All About the Youngest Jonas Brother Hollywood Life

July 04, 2024
blog
  • Frankie Jonas is the youngest sibling of the Jonas Brothers.
  • He worked with and appeared alongside the band as he was growing up.
  • His brothers shared sweet birthday messages for him on September 28, 2023.

Almost all millennials know that Kevin, Joe, and Nick Jonas make up the band the Jonas Brothers (uppercase “B”), but they’re not all of the Jonas brothers (lowercase “b”). While the trio make up the band, there is actually one more younger Jonas, and that’s Frankie. While he’s not a part of the band, it’s clear that he’s still incredibly close with the rest of his family. 

Frankie celebrated his 23rd birthday on Thursday, September 28, and his brothers all penned tributes to him to celebrate his special day. Nick, 31, shared a throwback photo of the two of them and wrote, “I love you very much” on his Instagram Story. Joe, 34, similarly posted an old pic and called his younger brother “one of the coolest people I know.” Kevin made a normal post to wish a happy birthday to “the man, the myth, the legend.” Following Frankie’s birthday, here’s everything you need to know about him!

Frankie Dropped His Debut EP in June

While he’s not in the band with his brothers, Frankie does have his own musical talent. He began his solo career back in February, releasing his debut single “Cocaine.” He performs under the name Franklin Jonas, and he released his debut 5-song EP Sewer Rat in June. The album is made up of alternative pop-rock, showing off his own musical tastes.

He Worked With his Brothers A Lot Growing Up

As a kid, Frankie was more of an actor, while his brother’s band became international superstars. He made his voice-acting debut in the English version of the 2008 film Ponyo. As the Jonas Brothers began film and TV projects after their hit albums, Frankie made appearances alongside his brothers. He had small roles in Camp Rock 2 and their Disney Channel series JONAS. 

He Co-Hosts ‘Claim to Fame’ with Kevin

All these years later, Frankie still gets to work with his older brothers on tons of exciting projects. Most notably, he co-hosts the reality series Claim to Fame with Kevin. The show features contestants who are all related to a different celebrity living in a house, and they’re each trying to figure out what star each person is related to. The brothers finished the second season at the end of August!

He’s Sober

While Frankie seems like he has a lot of fun with his many projects, he has been open about struggling with depression and substance use. He revealed having suicidal thoughts in the past in a March 2021 TikTok. “From a very young age, I struggled with drinking and drugging as an escape, ’cause I hated life, and I didn’t want to be here,” he said. “After many years of trying to kill myself accidentally, I came to a point where I was going to do it for real, and something intervened, and my life was saved.”

He revealed that after that experience, he got sober, and it has helped him immensely. “I went to treatment, and it saved me. I couldn’t be more grateful for the fact that I’m alive today, because my world has changed so beautifully and astronomically,” he said.

He’s a Student at Columbia University

While Frankie has been very open about his life on TikTok and seems excited to share his own music with the world, he has other interests, namely astrophysics. It was revealed that he was taking virtual classes on astrophysics (as well as academic writing) in a 2021 profile in Bustle. “I’m getting the best grades I’ve ever gotten because it’s actual material that I’m interested in,” he told the outlet.
