It’s about to get extra neon in Orlando.

On Nov. 10-12, the annual Electric Daisy Carnival — EDC, for short — will return to Tinker Field for three days of beat drops, dance and good times.

Just a few of this year’s headliners set to deliver the drops include Steve Aoki, deadmau5, The Chainsmokers, Gryffin and Zedd.

They’ll be joined by Alison Wonderland, San Holo, Paul Van Dyk, Kaskade and Dillon Francis.

In total, over 160 (!) acts will grace the six stages over the long weekend.

And if you want to get your EDC on from Friday through Sunday, it isn’t too late to grab your last-minute tickets.

At the time of publication, the lowest price we could find on three-day general admission passes was $271 before fees on Vivid Seats.

Single-day passes start at $144 before fees.
