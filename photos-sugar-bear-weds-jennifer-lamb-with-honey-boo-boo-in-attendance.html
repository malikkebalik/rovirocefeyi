PHOTOS Sugar Bear weds Jennifer Lamb with Honey Boo Boo in attendance

September 24, 2024
blog

Sugar Bear is officially hitched up y’all!

Photos of the late January wedding for Mike “Sugar Bear” Thompson and Jennifer Lamb have been shared via WeTV.

The star of Here Comes Honey Boo Boo and the father of the show’s breakout star, Alana Thompson, said “I do” in his back yard. The location was just steps from the double wide that he and ex-wife “Mama” June Shannon shared when Honey Boo Boo was a baby Boo Boo.

If you’re questioning the pair’s devotion, WeTV stated that the ceremony went off without any problems in spite of tornado warnings and heavy downpours.

Alana wore a pretty yellow dress with a denim jacket and carried a bouquet of baby’s breath as she escorted her daddy down the aisle.

So was Mama June in attendance? And how does she really feel about Sugar Bear’s new bride (if this social media exchange that occurred after the engagement is any indication it might not be good)? You’ll have to tune into the premiere of Mama June: From Not To Hot on WeTV airing February 24 at 10/9c to find out.
