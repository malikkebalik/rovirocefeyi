Doreen Dougherty Obituary – Cause of Death: In loving memory of Holy Redeemer Principal Doreen Dougherty.

Doreen Suzanne Dougherty of Shavertown, passed away peacefully at the age of 54 on Sunday morning, August 20, 2023, at home, surrounded by her loving family.

Doreen was born April 15, 1969, in Kingston, Doreen to Joseph Lipinski and Elaine Repak Lipinski. She was a graduate of Bishop O’Reilly High School, Class of 1987.

Doreen’s unwavering dedication to Catholic education drove her to become the building principal of All Saints Academy. In the fall of 2019, Doreen was proud to be named the principal of Holy Redeemer High School. Both All Saints Academy and Holy Redeemer High School served as her second homes, her obituary read.

Anyone who knew Doreen, knows family was everything to her. Doreen’s true love is her husband, Robert, with whom she would have celebrated 30 wonderful years of marriage in October.
