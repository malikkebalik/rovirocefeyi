Ọnye na-bụ Kristi Noem?

Kristi Noem, onye ndọrọ ndọrọ ọchịchị America, bụ onye ọchịchị 33 nke South Dakota ugbu a. Site na 2011 ruo 2019, ọ bụ onye nnọchi anya US na nnukwu ọgbakọ ọgbakọ South Dakota. Ọ rụkwara ọrụ dị ka onye otu South Dakota House of Representatives n'etiti 2007 na 2011. A họpụtara ya gọvanọ South Dakota na 2018 na nwanyị mbụ gọvanọ.

A mụrụ Kristi na Watertown, South Dakota, na 30th Nọvemba 1971. Ọ gbara 51 afọ dịka nke 2022.

A họpụtara Kristi ka ọ bụrụ Ụlọ Nnọchiteanya South Dakota na 2006 dịka onye Republican. Ọ nọchitere anya mpaghara 6th (Beadle Clark, Codington na Hamlin counties mana ọ bụghị Watertown). A họpụtara Kristi ọzọ n'ọkwa nke abụọ na 2008 na 41% nke votu. Kristi kwadoro ma kwado ego iji wedata afọ agụmakwụkwọ mmanye South Dakota ruo 16 na 2009 na 2010. E welitere nke a ruo 18 na 2008. Enwere esemokwu gbasara ma ịga ụlọ akwụkwọ na 18 ga-abawanye ọnụego ngụsị akwụkwọ.

Kristi meriri n'ọkwa nke Republican n'afọ 2010 site na vootu 42% megide Chris Nelson, odeakwụkwọ South Dakota, na Blake Curd, onye nnọchi anya steeti. A họpụtara Kristi ọzọ maka ọkwa nke abụọ na 2012, meriri Matthew Varilek site na 57%-43%.

2013 hụrụ mmeri ya Corrina Robinson, Democrat, na nke atọ. N'afọ 2014, a họpụtara Kristi ọzọ na nke anọ ma merie Paula Hawks, Democrat, na votu nke 64%-36%.
