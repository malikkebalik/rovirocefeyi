Many would find it hard to believe, but the gorgeous actress Zoe McLellan paved her path to the very top of the entertainment industry throughout the peak of her career.

The versatile actress often switches from television and movies back and forth to grace her fans in both platforms. So, without wasting any time, let us move forward to know how the dashing beauty made such great numbers.

Astonishing Net Worth of Five Millions

With more than two decades in the entertainment industry, the elegant personality accumulates a huge fortune of $5 millions. Alongside great wealth, she also gained a massive fan following via her tremendous work in television.

The leading diva is popular with her works in American crime investigation series NCIS and JAG.

Recurring Roles in JAG

Even though the promising talent debut in television in 1995 in Under Suspicion, her big break came when she was the cast of the renowned television drama JAG.

The actress portrayed the role of Petty officer Jennifer Coates. The role was a recurring one in season seven to season nine. However, she came to limelight when she had the main role in the much anticipated season ten of the series.

Roller-Coaster Journey in NCIS

Joey reunited with Donald P. Bellasario, producer of JAG when she signed with the popular action police procedure drama NCIS. Moreover, she was in two different editions of the series, NCIS and NCIS: New Orleans.

While she portrayed the role of Agent Meredith Brody in both, she featured in only two episodes of the former. She was promoted to the main role in the latter one for the first two seasons. In the sets of NCIS, she shared the stage with Sadie Calvano, Cote De Pablo, and William Devane.

Everything was going just well for Joe, until one day when she had to keep a balance between her personal and professional life. When she moved her dear son to Toronto to pursue her own career in Designated Survivor, her divorced husband accused her of child abduction. She won in the court proceedings and received the right to stay with her son. Within all this fuss, she left the show to move on and get a new fresh start.

Over Twenty Television Credits

Besides work in NCIS and JAG, Mclellan also featured in some episodes of the super hit series Suits and also in the medical drama House MD. She played as Sidney Merrick, a mystery patient in Hugh Laurie‘s classic.

The lady showed her versatility with different roles in Perception, The Whole Truth, Dirty Sexy Money, and a few more. In addition to that, she also acted in the all-time science-fiction hit, Star Trek: Voyager.

Journey on Big-Screens

Zoe McLellan’s first-ever on-screen action came when she was only 20 in Imaginary Crimes. Even though her initial on-screen roles were minor ones, she perfected the works given to her and moved on to bigger roles in Stonebrook, Dungeons & Dragons and Back to L.A.

The actress of such caliber and standard surely earns more than $50.99 per hour, the average salary of an actor in the States.

A Divorcee

The actress took a big step in her personal life back in February 2012, when she married fellow actor J.P Gillian. With a promising dream, the duo also shared a son, Sebastian Gillian in 2013.

But, suddenly a huge storm came in their way and the two had to part ways in 2015. Things were quite rough to end with since her husband accused her of child abduction. The 44-year old got divorced in 2016 and focused on their life then after.
