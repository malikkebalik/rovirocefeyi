'OutDaughtered's Adam Busby Posts Daughter Blake's Childhood Pics in Tribute on Her 10th B-Day

July 25, 2024
blog

"Outdaughtered" star Adam Busby celebrated his preteen daughter Blayke most sweetly, sharing lovely photos of her on her tenth birthday.

The oldest child of the "Outdaughtered" lot, Blayke Busby, recently reached a milestone age celebrating her 10th birthday. Her star dad, Adam Busby, took to Instagram, commemorating her new age.

Adam posted throwback images of the preteen girl as he reveled in fatherhood, showing their unique father-daughter relationship. Adam chronicled the photos right from when Blayke joined the Busby family.

The Busby family from "Outdaughtered" playing dress up | Photo: Getty Images

In the photo, the much younger Adam held up his first child, showing her off while they seemed to be at the hospital. The following slide included Blayke's growth progress into a toddler.

And this pattern continued as she advanced in age. In some of the snapshots, Adam and his wife, Danielle, featured, showing some of their playtimes with the tot. The doting dad of six gushed over his oldest child:

"I can't even put into words how proud I am to be your daddy. You ate my first baby. You made me a daddy. Happy 10th birthday, Blaykers…"

For the youngster's birthday party, Adam and Danielle decided to make the event as memorable as possible, even though the pandemic limited activities that Blayke could indulge.

It seems [Danielle and Adam] might just be looking to expand their family further.

While their safety was of the utmost importance, the celebrity couple got a few of Blayke's pals together because she hadn't seen them in a long time. They organized a fun sleepover and shared details on social media.

The TLC stars were set on making it a birthday to remember starting an early birthday celebration in Blayke's birthday week. This was as opposed to last year when she turned nine. At the time, not much could be done because of the pandemic.

Recently speaking on how their family-of-eight has been faring amid quarantine, social distancing, and the pandemic, fitness expert, Danielle, stated that her brood has had to adjust to a new routine since the pandemic was declared.

She mentioned that in the past, her family was mostly in a rush trying to get the day's activities started, but with the pandemic, the silver lining was the fact they had more family time.

It seems the multiple-birth stars might just be looking to expand their family further. Danielle and Adam, who are parents to six kids, including a quintuplet, talked about adoption possibilities.

Adam opened up on gender factors stating that he did not feel they could adopt a male child because of complications. However, the couple mentioned that their options are open with adoption in view.
